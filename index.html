The company was established in 2014 and since that time is an active internati onal coal trader. We supply coal to all continents, both to consumers as well as other trading companies. Krunch Ltd is an exclusive export trader for Razrez Kiyzassky LLC (Kiyzassky Open Cut), Razrez Vostochny LLC (Vostochny Open Cut). Razrez Kiyzassky LLC was established in November 2011. The mine is located near Novokuznetsk city, with greatly developed transport infrastructure – railway and automobile roads. Confirmed Reserves are 123 mln. tons of coal. Projected excavation of mine is 4500 mln. tones per annum. Transportation of coal from the mine is arranged by auto transport. Then coal is loaded in to the railway cars at Myski station. Station loading capacity is up to 20000 mt. per day. Nowadays Kizyassky mine is the biggest coal open-cut mine in Russia, which produce Low volatile coal.
